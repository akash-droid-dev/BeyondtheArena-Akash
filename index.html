§ 01 Manifesto

A career spent between
the whistle and the wireframe.

I work where sports operations and product meet. One week I'm authoring the accreditation policy for a multi-sport national event; the next I'm sitting with engineers to scope a registration module, or driving across a state to set up an archery range to AAI standards.

The throughline is the same: take a messy, high-stakes situation — a stadium, a federation, a state government — and turn it into something that runs. Documents, dashboards, workflows, vendor matrices, escalation paths. Whatever the system needs.

I've delivered for the IPL, the National Games, Khelo India, the Sports Authority of Jharkhand, and a state-level cultural event hosted by the Prime Minister. I write the BRDs. I print the badges. I'm comfortable in both rooms.
